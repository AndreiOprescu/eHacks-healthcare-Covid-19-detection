COVID-19 Predictor

Welcome to the COVID-19 Predictor! You can submit an X-ray image of your lung here, and we will use it to predict whether or not you have COVID-19.

Our website uses a machine learning algorithm to process the image which the computer will use to determine whether or not you have COVID-19!

Note that this software is not 100% accurate. It uses a range of probabilities to make its decision. This software is also not a substitute for a medical institute or doctor. Please check a clinic or hospital for details.
